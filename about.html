About Us

About Us

Welcome to Burien Bilingual Childcare, your trusted partner in bilingual early education, located in the heart of Burien, Washington.

Who We Are

We are a team of dedicated, experienced, and passionate educators committed to fostering a nurturing environment where children can grow and learn. Our staff is proficient in multiple languages, enabling us to provide a truly bilingual education.

What We Do

At Burien Bilingual, we offer a unique curriculum that seamlessly integrates language learning with daily activities. Our program is designed to immerse children in a second language, enhancing their cognitive development while also preparing them for a globalized world.

When We Operate

Our doors are open Monday through Friday, from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, providing flexible options to accommodate the busy schedules of modern families.

Where We Are

Conveniently located in Burien, we are easily accessible to families throughout the city. Our facility is designed with children in mind, providing a safe, engaging, and stimulating environment where they can thrive.
